class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">Instructions</div> <ol> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C) and set out a 9x13-inch baking dish or rimmed baking sheet. Also brush halved peppers with a neutral, high heat oil, such as grape seed, avocado or refined coconut. Set aside.</li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Prepare cauliflower rice by following <a href="http://minimalistbaker.com/how-to-make-cauliflower-rice/" target="_blank">these instructions</a>. Then heat a large rimmed skillet over medium heat.</li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Once hot, add oil, garlic (optional), onion, salt and pepper. Sauté for 1 minute, stirring frequently. Then add cauliflower ‘rice’ and stir to coat. Place the lid on to steam the rice for about 1 minute, then remove from heat and transfer the mixture to a large mixing bowl.You aren't looking to completely cook the 'rice', as it will continue cooking in the oven.</li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Add remaining ingredients - black beans through salt and pepper - to the cauliflower rice and mix to thoroughly combine. Taste and adjust seasonings accordingly, adding salt, pepper, or more spices as desired.</li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Generously stuff halved peppers with ~ 1/2 cup of the mixture until all peppers are full (you may have a little leftover filling, which can be added to salads or served on the side), then cover the dish with foil.</li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Bake for 30 minutes covered, then remove foil, increase heat to 400 degrees F (204 C), and bake for another 15-20 minutes, or until peppers are soft and slightly golden brown. For softer peppers, bake 5-10 minutes more. Serve with desired toppings (listed above) or as is. I recommend avocado, lime juice, hot sauce, and cilantro.</li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Best when fresh, though leftovers keep covered in the refrigerator for 2-3 days. Reheat in a 350 degree F (176 C) oven until warmed through - about 20 minutes.
